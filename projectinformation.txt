### Project 01: Predictive PTSD Early Warning System

**The Critical Problem:**
- 40% increase in mental health diagnoses among troops (2019-2023)
- 25% of PTSD patients drop out before treatment completion
- Less than 50% of veterans needing mental health care receive ANY treatment
- Traditional screening happens AFTER symptoms are severe
- Unit commanders have no early warning indicators

**Why Current Solutions Fail:**
- Annual screening isn't frequent enough to catch deterioration
- Self-reporting has massive stigma barrier
- No predictive capability - only reactive
- Clinical appointments have 30-60 day wait times
- No integration between training stress data and mental health

**What This System Does:**
Multi-modal passive monitoring that predicts mental health decline 30-90 days before crisis, enabling early intervention without stigma.

**Core Innovation:**
Analyzes digital footprints without requiring self-disclosure:
- Physical fitness test score trends (sudden decline indicates stress)
- Sleep pattern changes from fitness trackers
- Social interaction patterns (isolation indicators)
- Training performance degradation
- Medical appointment frequency changes
- Peer wellness check-in sentiment analysis
- Deployment cycle stress mapping
- Family separation duration correlation

**Technical Architecture:**
- **Data Ingestion**: Secure API integration with military health systems, training databases, fitness tracking
- **ML Model**: Gradient boosting ensemble with LSTM for time-series patterns
- **Privacy Layer**: Federated learning - data never leaves local installation
- **Alert System**: Tiered alerts (green/yellow/red) to unit leaders and mental health coordinators
- **Intervention Engine**: Connects high-risk individuals with resources automatically
- **Dashboard**: Commander view showing unit mental health trending

**Key Features:**
1. **Passive Monitoring**: No surveys or self-reporting required
2. **Privacy-Preserving**: Uses aggregated patterns, not individual surveillance
3. **Actionable Alerts**: Specific intervention recommendations per risk level
4. **Unit-Level Analytics**: Identify systemic stressors affecting entire units
5. **Outcome Tracking**: Measure intervention effectiveness
6. **Predictive Timeline**: 30/60/90-day risk forecasting
7. **Resource Integration**: Auto-connect to counseling, peer support, chaplain services
8. **Stigma-Free**: Framed as "readiness enhancement" not "mental illness detection"

**Tech Stack:**
- **Backend**: Python, FastAPI, PostgreSQL (with encryption at rest)
- **ML**: TensorFlow, Scikit-learn, XGBoost
- **Data Pipeline**: Apache Airflow for ETL
- **Frontend**: React dashboard for commanders
- **Mobile**: React Native app for wellness check-ins
- **Security**: FIPS 140-2 compliant encryption, HIPAA-aligned data handling
- **Deployment**: Kubernetes on DoD secure cloud

**Impact Metrics:**
- Reduce suicide attempts by 40% through early intervention
- Increase treatment engagement from 50% to 75%
- Reduce treatment dropout from 25% to 10%
- Decrease commander surprise mental health crises by 60%

**Difficulty**: HARD
**Estimated Scope**: 18-22 files, 2500-3000 lines, 4-6 months development
